Anjuna Beach in North Goa

Anjuna Beach is famous for its rave parties, flea market, and rocky shoreline. It attracts many foreign travellers every year for its Instagram-worthy looks.

  • Best time to visit- November to February
  • How to reach-
    • By Air: Goa International Airport is at a distance of 47 km, from where you can hire a car.
    • By Train: Vasco da Gama Railhead is 47 km from the beach.
    • By Road: Anjuna Beach is 10 km from the Mapusa Bus Stand, and cabs and autos can help you cover the distance.
